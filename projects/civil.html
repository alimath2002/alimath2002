Architectural Design

During my architecture course at Sharif University of Technology, we were assigned to draw plans and sections for a given project based on site conditions. Compliance with building construction laws was required. The project involved designing a building consisting of a basement, a lobby, and two residential floors. I received a high grade for this project.

Client

architecture instructor

Date

2025

Category

architecture

Technologies

AutoCAD

Project Features

  • interior design
  • designed according to site weather properties
  • fire precautions were considered like emergency exit
  • convenient use for disabled people
  • green environments were well positioned

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenges were the open design and space limitations, especially along the width. By placing rooms on the southern s ide, sunlight absorption was increased. Additionally, a proper stair box was placed adjacent to the ramp to minimize unused spaces.
